If you're still interested (which you should be), what these numbers are saying is that in Ontario from 2017-2018, the total number of all ED visits were ~6.4M and out of those, ~1.9M of visits were from frequent ED users. The National Ambulatory Care Reporting System defines "Frequent ED user: Patient with four or more ED visits in the fiscal year".

More than 1/4 of Ontario's Emergency Department (ED) visits were from an individual who's been there 4 times or more.
