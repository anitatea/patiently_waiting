Stay tuned for a cool visual... To boil it down, in Ontario, the total number of all ED visits were ~6.4M and out of those, ~1.9M of visits were from frequent ED users. The National Ambulatory Care Reporting System defines "Frequent ED user: Patient with four or more ED visits in the fiscal year".
